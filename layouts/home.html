York/Sheridan Program in Design
presents The Intermission,
the 2016 grad show.

To see the works and meet the cast, come check out our show, The Intermission, on April 19th at The Libery Grand.

Show Information

The YSDN Grad Show celebrates our last four years of design studies and accomplishments, and share our hopes and fears for the future. In the theatre world, this break in the action is called "the intermission". It is a chance for us to break character from the many roles we must adopt as designers and take a moment to truly introduce ourselves.

Look how far we've come and see the possibilities that lie ahead. Welcome to The Intermission.
